Beef Wellington

Description

Recipe passed down by dukes and dutchesses from the Sucma Isles.

Extremely classy dish best served with a side of Ligma over rice.

Ingredients

  • A tooth from an African shaman
  • Beef
  • Wellington

Steps

  1. Grind the African shaman's tooth into a tasty paste
  2. Go to Kroger and purchase USDA grade sirloin
  3. Mix in Dutch oven for 45 minutes or until the wellington has hardened around the tooth.
